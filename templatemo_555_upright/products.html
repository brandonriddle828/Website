This is a cordless drill/driver with a high-performance brushless motor that provides 340 unit watts outs and measures only 6.3 in. front to rear (UWO). The drill/driver also has an ergonomic design for better tool control and balance, as well as an LED light for better illumination in dark areas and a retractable belt hook.
